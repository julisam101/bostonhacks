🔴 Red Line

⏳ $2.40

The longest line in the MBTA system, running north-south through Boston.

Major Stops:

  • Alewife (North)
  • Harvard Square
  • Kendall/MIT
  • Park Street
  • Downtown Crossing
  • South Station
  • JFK/UMass
  • Braintree (South)

✨ Connects to major universities and downtown Boston

🔵 Blue Line

⏳ $2.40

Connects Logan Airport to downtown Boston, great for travelers.

Major Stops:

  • Logan Airport (Terminals A, B, C)
  • Aquarium
  • Government Center
  • Bowdoin
  • Charles/MGH
  • Wonderland

✨ Direct access to Boston Logan Airport

🟠 Orange Line

⏳ $2.40

Runs through west and southwest Boston, connecting communities.

Major Stops:

  • Oak Grove (North)
  • Sullivan Square
  • Community College
  • Downtown Crossing
  • Back Bay
  • Stony Brook
  • Forest Hills (South)

✨ Connects back Bay and downtown shopping areas

💚 Green Line

⏳ $2.40

Light rail system with four branches serving western Boston.

Major Stops:

  • Boston College (B)
  • Copley (B)
  • Park Street
  • Government Center
  • Prudential
  • Riverside (D)
  • Cleveland Circle (C)
  • Museum of Science (E)

✨ Great for shopping and museums

⚪ Silver Line

⏳ $2.40

Bus rapid transit connecting Dudley Square and Logan Airport.

Major Stops:

  • Dudley Square (South)

  • Logan Airport (North)

✨ Newest line connecting airport and downtown
