## Boston Hacks 

- What it does: 
    - A location-based guide for the Boston area that helps users find their bearings, discover nearby points of interest, and get from point A to point B, all without switching between multiple apps. 

- How to use it: 
    1. Open the website and choose your starting location
    2. Choose your desintation from the second dropdown 
    3. Hit "Find My Route" to generate your route 
    4. From there you can also browse T Lines for transit info, check out Attractions or go back to the Main Page

- Link to Live Site: https://julisam101.github.io/bostonhacks/
